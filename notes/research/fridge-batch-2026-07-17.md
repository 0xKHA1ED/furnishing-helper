## How to read the two layouts (for you)

| | **Top freezer** (freezer on top) | **Bottom freezer** (fridge at eye level) |
|--|----------------------------------|------------------------------------------|
| Daily use | Bend for ice cream/meat; fridge is huge open space | Grab prep containers without bending; freezer in drawers |
| Meal-prep Friday | Great fridge shelves for trays | Drawers good for **organized** frozen packs (flat bags) |
| “Freeze a lot” | Total liters often higher for the money; freezer can still be only ~90–105 L | Freezer often ~105–115 L but total machine smaller (~340 L class) |
| Looks | Classic appliance | Often reads more “modern / premium” |
| Your hard 35k | Many 400–500 L class options | Samsung RB34 / Beko ~367 class |

**Net vs marketing liters:** Egypt listings often quote **gross** (bigger). Agents found e.g. Ariston “420” ≈ **367 L net**; Ariston “455” ≈ **406–408 L net**. Always compare **net** and **freezer split** when possible.
